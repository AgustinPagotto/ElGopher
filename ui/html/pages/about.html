About El Gopher

A personal space to share my projects, and articles that i have written

Who am I?

I'm Agustin Oliveros a developer from Argentina, with an obvious love for GO, I created this page to write about my journey from Front/Mobile development to newer things, in this case backend with GO; My projects, and maybe in the future my own articles.

Tech Stack

  • Go for backend logic and templating
  • HTMX for lightweight interactions
  • PostgreSQL for persistence

Why El Gopher?

Go was the language that really brought back my interest in learning how to properly use a programming language, read books about it and just play around with new projects. That is why i wanted to pay some respect to the language by using the Gopher name, also the "El" part is just "The" but in spanish, andsince my primary language is spanish i liked the word play.
