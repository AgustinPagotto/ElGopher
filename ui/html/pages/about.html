About El Gopher

A small development corner built with Go, HTMX, and PostgreSQL.

What is El Gopher?

El Gopher is my personal space on the internet — a place to share articles, projects, and experiments as I learn and build with Go. It’s intentionally simple, fast, and minimal.

Tech Stack

  • Go for backend logic and templating
  • HTMX for lightweight interactions
  • PostgreSQL for persistence

Who am I?

I'm a developer from Argentina, exploring backend development, Go, and building things that feel good to use.
